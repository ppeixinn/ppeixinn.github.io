Accessible Movie Narrator

Company Project - Private

Accessible Movie Narrator 无障碍电影分析器

This full-stack accessibility platform processes videos and generates AI-powered visual descriptions and narrations. The system employs multimodal AI analysis combining speech recognition (Whisper/Tencent ASR), visual understanding (Baidu vision models), and context-aware LLM generation to provide accurate, pedagogically-sound narrative guidance. The platform supports online video sources from Bilibili and Tencent Video, multiple AI backends (ZhipuQingyan, Tencent QianFan, Baidu), and offers both editing capabilities and batch processing features.

  • React + Flask
  • Multimodal AI
  • ASR
  • LLM integration
  • TTS
  • FFMPEG
